About Crafty Flips

Crafty Flips is a private web-based tool used to manage and publish short craft tutorial videos to social media platforms such as TikTok.

The platform helps streamline content posting workflows for short-form educational craft videos. It is designed for use by the account owner only and is not a public-facing service.
